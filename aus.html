Australia

Where should I travel?

About 10 million tourists visit Sydney and Melbourne every year. The city's landmarks are magnificent and seem to give off a sense of hope and confidence. But the landmarks aren't the only reason to visit Australia - the food is also delicious! The continent's star meals definitely take their forms as deserts. The pavlova and lamington cakes will draw in quite a crowd.
