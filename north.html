Thyme to Travel is a website dedicated to spreading our love of food, travel, and culture to others. We have many ideas of places to visit, restaurants to go, and food to eat and make! We hope that we can help you create new experiences.

Places to Visit

NYC & Los Angeles

New York and Los Angeles get an average of 62.8 million visitors a year. These states have many once in a life time attractions, such as The Empire State Building, The Statue of Liberty, and The Hollywood Sign. Not to mention the amazing foods! Foods can go from amazing apple pies to warm delicious cheese burgers or mouth watering steaks.

Mexico: Mexico City and Cozumel are both amazing places and reach up to 40 million tourist a year. The magnificent beaches and historic landmarks are just a few of the reasons that Cozumel and Mexico City are such popular areas in the world. The food is an amazing reason to visit too.
